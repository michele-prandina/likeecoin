Your likes, your wallet

What is it?

It is a digital currency based on the Facebook likes received from your friends.

Why should I use it?

Likes have an intrinsic value recognised by any facebook user that received them. An electronic commodity with such a high diffusion and recognised value it’s perfect to became a medium of exchange.

Ok, how it works?

An algorithm controls the exchange rate between the likes you collect everyday and the Likees creation.

Principles

HAPPINESS ECONOMICS

The number of likes that you receive is a measure of the emotional value that you were able to create. Making these “generated emotions” tradable is an incredible way to revolutionize the economic system.

PEOLPLE HAVE THE POWER

The first monetary system in history where currency is issued anytime you like something

DEMOCRATIC WEALTH

Anyone having a Facebook account and some friends can join the community. There are no privileges, dominant positions, discriminations or entry barriers.
